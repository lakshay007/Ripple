RIPPLE

Charging your way into the future


The major goal is to create span type 3 DC chargers for electric vehicles. The primary selling point of this project is its replacement of the traditional DC to DC converter, the specifics of which are given in the concept and objective section.

In addition to its fundamental technology, this charger will have a touchscreen that will allow consumers to pay online and monitor how long it will take a vehicle to charge completely. Users will also receive a mobile app, which they may use to locate the closest charger and monitor every aspect of charging even when they are not near their car.
